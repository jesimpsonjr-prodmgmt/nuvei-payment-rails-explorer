These four KPIs map directly to the role's requirement to "monitor product KPIs and metrics." Volume tracks growth. Approval rate measures conversion health. Settlement time reflects the rail mix (more real-time = lower blended average). Return rate is the risk signal. A rising return rate on ACH may indicate the need to shift deposit volume toward irrevocable rails like RTP/FedNow, or to implement Nuvei's Assured Funds product for risk mitigation.
